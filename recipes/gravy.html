Sausage Gravy

Description

Nothing pairs better with southern biscuits than good 'ol sausage gravy

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Pork Sausage

Steps

  1. Heat skillet on med-high heat to cook sausage. When properly browned, remove sausage with slotted spoon and place on a paper towel on a plate to drain.
  2. Slowly whisk flour into sausage fat in skillet
  3. Slowly whisk in milk to flour mixture
  4. Remove from heat and serve
