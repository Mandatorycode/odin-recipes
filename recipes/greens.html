Collard Greens

Description

How to cook southern style collard greens with maximum flavor... Just burn them a little

Ingredients

  • Collard Greens
  • Half a slice of cooked bacon cut into pieces

Steps

  1. Fill a 5 qt. pot 1/2 way with water
  2. Add collard greens and bacon
  3. Bring to a boil, then turn to med-low and cover
  4. Cook for 4 hours or until no liquid remains
